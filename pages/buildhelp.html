Build Help

Building a gaming PC can be daunting—here are a few top‐level tips:

  • Choose a motherboard that fits your case form-factor (ATX/mATX/ITX).
  • Balance your CPU and GPU so neither bottlenecks the other.
  • Invest in quality cooling (air or AIO) if you plan to overclock.
  • Make sure your PSU has enough wattage and the right connectors.
